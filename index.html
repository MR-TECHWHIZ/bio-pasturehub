OUR STORY

Bio-Pasture Hub is a Tanzanian-based agritech venture transforming livestock farming through sustainable feed production and waste management solutions. We specialize in hydroponic green fodder production and Black Soldier Fly (BSF) farming, providing farmers with nutritious, affordable, and eco-friendly animal feed alternatives.We combine technology, innovation, and environmental responsibility to solve two major agricultural challenges rising livestock feed costs and organic waste pollution. Our model empowers farmers with modern tools and knowledge to achieve higher productivity, profitability, and resilience in the face of climate change.

Our Vision

To become East Africa's leading provider of sustainable livestock feed solutions, empowering farmers and agribusinesses through innovation, green technology, and smart agriculture.

Our Mission

To provide affordable, high-quality, and sustainable animal feed by integrating hydroponic and insect-based technologies, while educating and equipping farmers to adopt climate-smart agricultural practices.

OUR SERVICES

We specialize in sustainable feed solutions and agricultural training.

Hydroponic Fodder Production

We grow fresh, nutritious fodder from grains using hydroponic systems a soil-less method that uses up to 90% less water and produces ready-to-feed grass in just 7 days.

  • Grown under controlled conditions
  • Chemical-free and rich in nutrients
  • Ideal for dairy cows, goats, sheep, and poultry
  • Increases milk yield and animal health

Black Soldier Fly (BSF) Farming

Our BSF program turns organic waste into high-protein larvae meal and organic fertilizer.

  • BSF larvae contain up to 45% protein and 35% fat
  • Frass (insect compost) improves soil fertility naturally
  • Reduces waste and greenhouse gas emissions

Training and Capacity Building

We train farmers, youth, and agripreneurs on hydroponic fodder cultivation, BSF rearing, and agribusiness operations.

Our goal is to create self-sufficient farmers who can produce their own feed while reducing environmental impact.

System Design and Installation

We design and install customized hydroponic and BSF systems from small household setups to large-scale commercial units with full technical support.

OUR APPROACH

How Our Model Works

A circular economy approach ensuring zero waste, reduced feed costs, and consistent income generation.

Waste Collection

We collect or receive organic waste from local farms, markets, or households.

BSF Conversion

The waste is converted into BSF larvae for protein meal and organic fertilizer.

Fodder Growth

Hydroponic systems produce daily green fodder using minimal land and water.

Feed Distribution

Farmers receive ready-made fodder or set up their own systems through our guidance.

Training & Expansion

We train new farmers and institutions to adopt our sustainable model.
